Carl Boettiger
UC Santa Cruz

Three curses of dimensionality

  1. State space
  2. Action space
  3. Outcome space

Solve:

$$Vt(S_t) = \max{xt \in \chi_t} \left(C(S_t, x_t) + \gamma \sum{s{\prime} \in \mathcal{S}} \mathbb{P}(s{\prime} | Stn, x_t) V{t+1}{n-1} s{\prime} \right)$$

and let $xn_t$ be the value of $x_t$ that solves the maximization (argmax).
